ABOUT US BASED IN THE HEART OF GALWAY CITY A long alley in the yard, a few flowers, a coffee table, a borrowed coffee
machine and two chairs under the inscription Jungle Cafe. That’s how we
started in April 2012. I, Daniel, aimed to start a business where people will
meet, taste original dishes, drink quality and fragrant coffee, and above all,
where they will feel good.

I relied on my 20-year year experience as a qualified chef, a passion for
coffee, and perhaps, empathy and love for good people. From my travels
in South America, I found my desire to be surrounded by nature, flowers
and plants, and wanted to put this desire into a cafe, and that’s how the
jungle cafe was born.

Jungle Cafe is proud and fortunate to have a team from all over the
world. Team members with an open heart, and who agreed with my vision
of a “good business”, enabling us to enrich each other – both humanly
and professionally. The team, who I am so grateful for, share the same
values as me, and that has helped us build the quality and reputation of
our cafe.

flavours, and enjoy the relaxed and informal environment of Jungle Cafe. GET IN TOUCH
